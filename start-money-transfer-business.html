The 8 Things Every Money Transfer Business Needs

Most people only think about licensing. In reality, launching a compliant, profitable remittance business requires 8 layers — all of which RemitSo provides.

01
Regulatory Coverage
You must be authorised to move money. Either operate under a licensed partner's coverage (no licence needed from you) or get your own. RemitSo connects you to regulated partners in USA, UK, Australia, Canada, and Europe.
02
White-Label Technology
Mobile apps (iOS & Android), a web portal, and a sender-facing interface — all branded to your business. Published under your developer accounts. Your customers never see RemitSo.
03
Back Office
FX rate engine, customer 360 profiles, transaction lifecycle management (21 states), treasury monitoring, role-based access, and audit logs ready for regulators on demand.
04
Payment Gateways
How senders fund transfers. Card processing, bank transfer, open banking, direct debit, NPP/PayID (Australia), Faster Payments (UK), ACH (USA). 8 gateways pre-integrated.
05
Payout Partners
How money reaches recipients in 100+ countries. Bank transfer, mobile wallets (bKash, M-Pesa, UPI, GCash, Airtel Money), cash pickup networks, and agent locations — all pre-integrated.
06
KYC Providers
Digital identity verification — document check, liveness detection, biometric match, and address verification. 3 tier-1 KYC providers integrated. 2-minute onboarding for your senders.
07
Compliance Engine
AML risk scoring (55+ factors), real-time sanctions screening (OFAC, UN, EU, UK, AUSTRAC lists), transaction monitoring rules, automated SAR/SMR filing. 97% of transactions auto-clear.
08
Fraud Prevention
55+ risk indicators, velocity checks, device fingerprinting, IP analysis, beneficiary risk scoring, and pattern detection. Flags suspicious activity before funds move — not after.

The Complete Stack

Every Layer of Your Business — Pre-Built

RemitSo isn't just software. It's the complete infrastructure a money transfer business needs to operate from day one.

📱 Customer-Facing Products
iOS & Android Apps
Native mobile apps published under your developer accounts on App Store and Google Play. Your logo, colours, name. Biometric login, live FX rates, 3-tap transfers, push notifications.
Web Portal
Fully branded web interface for senders who prefer desktop. Hosted on your domain. Same features as mobile — rate calculator, transaction history, beneficiary management.
Agent / Teller Interface
Walk-in agent interface for cash-based senders. Manage agents, process over-the-counter transactions, and track agent performance — all within the same platform.
🖥️ Operations & Back Office
FX & Pricing Engine
Set dynamic spreads per corridor, per customer tier, and per transfer size. Real-time FX rate display with live market feeds. You control every margin — and keep 100% of the spread.
Customer 360
Full customer profiles — KYC status, risk tier, transaction history, beneficiaries, notes, and alerts. Everything your compliance and ops teams need in a single view.
Treasury & Liquidity
Real-time balance monitoring per corridor and currency. Prefunding alerts, settlement tracking, and nostro account management to keep your liquidity healthy at all times.
🛡️ Compliance & Risk Engine
KYC — 3 Providers
Document verification, liveness detection, biometric match, and address check. 2-minute digital onboarding. Tier-1 providers pre-integrated.
AML & Risk Scoring
55+ configurable risk factors. Real-time scoring on every transaction. 97% auto-cleared without human review. Exceptions escalated to your team.
Sanctions Screening
OFAC, UN, EU, UK, and local watchlists (AUSTRAC, DFAT, FinCEN) on every transaction. 40,000+ entries. Sub-second screening on sender, beneficiary, and institution.
SAR / SMR Filing
One-click suspicious activity reporting in the format required by your regulator — FinCEN (SAR), AUSTRAC (SMR), NCA (UK SAR), FINTRAC (STR). Full audit trail included.
💳 Payment Gateways — How Senders Fund Transfers
Card Processing
Visa and Mastercard debit/credit card payments. 3DS2 authentication. Multiple acquirers available per market. Instant funding for time-sensitive transfers.
Bank Transfer & Open Banking
ACH (USA), SEPA (EU), Faster Payments (UK), NPP/PayID/Osko (Australia), BPAY. Open Banking via PSD2 APIs. Lower cost than card, high trust for senders.
Direct Debit
Scheduled and recurring transfers via direct debit mandates. Ideal for regular remitters — workers sending home monthly. Available in UK, Australia, and Europe.
🌍 Payout Partners — How Recipients Get Paid
Bank Transfer
Direct to bank accounts in 100+ countries. SWIFT, local ACH, IMPS (India), INSTAPAY (Philippines), BACS (UK). Next-day or real-time settlement available.
Mobile Wallets
bKash (Bangladesh), M-Pesa (Kenya/Tanzania), GCash & Maya (Philippines), UPI (India), Airtel Money, MTN MoMo, Wave (West Africa). Instant delivery to wallet.
Cash Pickup
Agent networks in Africa, Asia, and Latin America. Recipients pick up cash at a local agent using a PIN. Covers areas with low banking penetration.
Home Delivery
Cash delivered to the recipient's home in select markets including India, Philippines, and parts of Africa. Available via our payout partner network.
