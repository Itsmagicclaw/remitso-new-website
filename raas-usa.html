Why RaaS Makes Sense

The Commercial Logic
is Compelling.

Building your own FinCEN licence costs 12–18 months and hundreds of thousands in legal fees. Here's the numbers.

Time to Market
4–8 Weeks vs. 12–18 Months
Obtaining your own FinCEN licence takes 12–18 months minimum — legal fees, regulatory engagement, compliance infrastructure. RaaS gets you live in 4–8 weeks under our existing licence.
Zero Revenue Share
Keep 100% of your FX spreads.
Other RaaS providers take 0.5–2% of every transaction. At any meaningful volume, that's tens of thousands of dollars per month lost. RemitSo charges a flat fee only — you keep everything you earn.
Brand Continuity
Your customers see only your brand.
Customers interact entirely with your brand — not ours. When you graduate to your own licence, the transition is seamless. All customer data, history, and relationships belong to you from day one.
Fixed Compliance Cost
Predictable cost regardless of volume.
Unlike revenue-share models where compliance costs grow with your volume, RaaS charges a flat fee. Your cost structure is fixed and predictable — critical for early-stage financial planning.
Upgrade Path
Build your licence in parallel.
While running on RaaS, you build your customer base and revenue. We support your own licence application when you're ready — technology transfer, compliance documentation, regulatory introductions.
FATF Recognised
Globally compliant model.
FATF (Financial Action Task Force) globally recognises the sponsor/agent model as a compliant structure for remittance services. You're operating within a well-established, accepted framework.
